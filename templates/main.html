Drishti

Where the eyes go, the mind follows

An effort towards a more secure world.

It is a centralized solution that will analyze data from multiple sources. It is capable of detecting a person's face and vehicles in real time and automatically matching it with the database. Additionally, it is capable of detecting criminal activity/crime with CCTV by evaluating live movies for violence, mob formation, suspicious body language, etc.

Some numbers

  • Around 1.54 million cameras are installed in the top 15 cities of India
  • New Delhi ranks first with 1,826.58 cameras per square mile
  • Cities like New Delhi (59.54), Indore (49.88), Hyderabad (43.78) and Chennai (40.87), which incidentally are also the focal points of CCTV surveillance in the country, also rank high in the crime index

Our solution, Drishti, can help reduce manual work. It is capable of processing the feeds of all CCTV cameras installed in public and semi-public premises for Police Control Rooms/Centralized Command and Control Centre.
